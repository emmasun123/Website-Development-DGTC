Identifying AI text and voices

Chat GPT is widely used around the world by many people, including students and teachers, even bots on the internet. Sometimes Chat GPT and other Large Language Models can be wrong, spreading misinformation. Occasionally AI detection tools are wrong as well, so it is useful to learn the patterns of AI text. AI generated voices have also gained traction, and has had news stories circulating of elderly getting scammed by being misled by this technology, thinking that their loved one is in danger after they hear their voice on the phone. Being aware of these can decrease misinformation on the internet.
