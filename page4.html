Identifying AI images and videos

Introduction

AI images and videos can be both easy and difficult to identify due to the evolution of generative AI, so it is important to learn AI identifying skills. Some images and videos are obviously AI, while others require a closer look.

'All eyes on Rafah' image

With over 45 million shares on instagram, this image is one of the most widespread AI generated images. This image was first posted by @shahv4012, following an air strike by Israel. This image's non-confronting nature has bypassed social media censorship, which helps explain its popularity.
