Identifying AI images and videos

AI images and videos can be both easy and difficult to identify due to the evolution of generative AI, so it is important to learn AI identifying skills. Some images and videos are obviously AI, while others require a closer look.





Identifying AI generated images

Grandma encased in resin

It should be obvious that this image of a grandma encased in resin is fake at first glance. There are inconsistencies in the hands, and who would actually put a grandma in resin?

Donald Trump getting arrested

The police officers faces are deformed, and theres a big unidentifiable lump of mass in the center. The clothes don't hang in the right way and the shoes are deformed.

AI storybook art

By looking closely at AI art and thinking about each thought that goes into each stroke of a stylus, you can easily determine between AI and real art. In this example, an artist would never draw hands like that, make the hair merge into the neck, draw plants with no clear shape, and draw unidentifiable objects in a character's hair.




Identifying AI generated videos
